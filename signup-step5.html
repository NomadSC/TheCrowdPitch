  • Under Title III - Regulation Crowdfunding, companies can raise a maximum of $1M in a 12 month period.
  • All age 18 and over accredited and non-acredited investors can invest in this offering.
  • The investor limits are based on annual investments.They are determined by net worth, annual income and other Title III investments during that time.
  • The shares are restricted and cannot be sold for a minimum of one year from purchase. even after this time,there may be no market resale available.
  • Vist our learn page for more information.
  • Under Title IV - Regulation A+, companies can raise a maximum of $50M in a 12 month period.
  • All age 18 and over accredited and non-acredited investors can invest in this offering.
  • The investments limits are per deal, not an annual aggregate. These limitations are determined by net worth, annual income and accreditation status.
  • The investors can seel their shares at any point after the offering has closed, although there may be a limited or no trading market which will impact your ability to sell shares.
  • Title IV - Regulation A+ has two tiers under which companies canraise capitol. To learn more about these, visit our education page.
  • Once a company chooses to launch a Title IV Test the Waters Campaign, they cannot move to a Title III - Regulation Crowdfunding campaign.
